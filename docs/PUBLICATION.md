# Suggested publication positioning

Suggested title: **A Canonical Stieltjes–Jacobi Realization of the Primitive Riemann Xi Resolvent: Finite Zero Reconstruction and Preregistered GUE Tests**

Position the contribution as a canonical, unfitted finite realization and reproducible computational study. Avoid claims of an RH proof, a completed Hilbert–Pólya construction, or global GUE universality.

The main analytic target is an unconditional theorem of the form

\[
R(u)=\int_0^\infty\frac{d\pi(x)}{1-ux},\qquad d\pi(x)\ge0,
\]

with determinacy and self-adjointness of the corresponding multiplication/Jacobi operator.
